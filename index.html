My Projects

SlaughterHouse

The SlaughterHouse is a top-down puzzle and horror game that combines exploration and the terror of being chased to rush the player to escape the haunted house of the monster that kidnapped them. The player runs through a village house where they discover various frightening things.

Assembly Lines

Assembly Lines is a puzzle video game where the player must connect machines with different functions to create an assembly line that produces the required parts. The challenge lies in organizing the machines logically and efficiently, respecting the necessary production order.
